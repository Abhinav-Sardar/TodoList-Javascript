Here's a guide on how to use this app.

  1. In order to add a task , type your task in the text field
  2. Click on the "Add Task" button to add your specified task
  3. Click on the "Trash" button to remove a task
  4. If you want to clear all your taks , click on the "Clear All" button
  5. Tasks are stored locally on your device
  6. If you close your browser and revisit the page , your tasks will be intact
  7. They will only be deleted through the trash or clear button
